## Description

```
Lena is a simple, private app that helps you nurture the friendships that matter most.

HOW IT WORKS

Import your contacts, organize friends into tiers (Clique, Good Friends, Catch-up), and Lena takes care of the rest. You'll get gentle reminders to reach out when it's been too long, and you'll never miss a birthday again.

FEATURES

- Birthday reminders — Get notified on the day, so you're always the friend who remembers.
- Smart check-in suggestions — Lena tracks when you last caught up and nudges you when it's time.
- Quick interaction logging — After a call, coffee, or text, log it in seconds.
- Friend tiers — Prioritize your inner circle. Set different reminder frequencies for different groups.
- Contact import — Pull names, birthdays, and photos straight from your iPhone contacts.
- Timeline view — See who needs attention today at a glance.

PRIVACY FIRST

All your data stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking. Lena doesn't send anything anywhere. Your friendships are your business.

Lena is for people who care about their relationships but need a little help staying consistent. Because the best friendships don't happen by accident.
```
