Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Lena ("the App") is a personal friend relationship manager built by Francesco Pontrandolfo. Your privacy matters, and the App is designed to keep your data private by default.

Data We Collect

Lena stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Contact information — Names, phone numbers, birthdays, and photos imported from your iPhone Contacts, with your permission.
  • Interaction notes — Short notes you write when you log a catch-up with a friend.
  • App settings — Your notification preferences, reminder times, and contact frequency settings.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party. There is no account system, no analytics, and no tracking.

Permissions We Request

  • Contacts — To import friend names, birthdays, and photos from your iPhone Contacts. You can use the app without granting this permission.
  • Photos — To let you set custom profile pictures for friends.
  • Notifications — To send you birthday and check-in reminders. All notifications are scheduled locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Lena does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The App does not make network requests to external servers.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data lives on your device for as long as you use the App. Deleting the App removes all stored data permanently. There is no cloud backup.

Children's Privacy

Lena is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
